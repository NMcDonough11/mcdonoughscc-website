Golf.
Brotherhood.
Purpose.

This isn't golf.
This is a movement.

What started with 36 guys and a dream in 2024 has become Calgary's most talked-about pre-Stampede event. In 2025, we brought 72 players together for 36 holes. So far, we've raised over $20,000 for Wood's Homes, and proved that guys showing up for each other can change everything.

In 2026, we're going bigger. 120 players. 36 holes. One mission: supporting men's wellness and showing up for the guys in our lives.

$310 Per player

Why $310?

Physical activity boosts mental health—and it's even better when you do it with the guys in your corner. The World Health Organization recommends 300 minutes of physical activity per week for maximum health benefits. A round of golf checks that box. The extra $10 goes directly to Wood's Homes.

36 holes. Zero excuses.

Supporting Wood's Homes

After tournament costs, proceeds go to Wood's Homes in support of men's wellness programs. Wood's Homes is a mental health centre providing treatment and support for children, young people, adults and families. New this year, we'll be raising money for a specific project that otherwise wouldn't happen for Wood's Homes. Our goal is to make a tangible impact in the community, and that project will be announced in the next few weeks. Your $310 doesn't just buy you a day of golf — it changes lives.
