# Design Context

## Target Audience
Young professional men in Calgary (25-40). Golfers, business leaders, community builders. They want to have the best day of their summer — competitive golf, great food, party bus, afterparty. They're social, connected, and will share on Instagram.

## Use Cases
1. Register for the tournament ($310 per person)
2. Learn what's included and see the schedule
3. Become a sponsor (businesses wanting brand exposure)
4. Sign up to volunteer
5. Get hyped about the event — FOMO-driven conversion

## Brand Personality / Tone
"Calgary Stampede meets the Masters, but it knows how to party."
- Western swagger — cowboy hats, boots, bold and unapologetic
- Premium golf tournament energy — polished, not cheap
- Confident, fun, a little cocky — never corporate, never boring
- Think rodeo posters meets luxury event branding

## Aesthetic Direction
- **Tone**: Western maximalist luxury — bold red, black, gold accents on cream
- **Typography**: Rye (chunky Western serif) for impact headers, Poppins (clean sans) for body
- **Colors**: #C8102E red, #1A1A1A black, #FAF3E8 cream, #C5A55A gold
- **Textures**: Diagonal red stripes, subtle Western patterns
- **Differentiation**: The countdown timer, "Why $310?" callout, and the bold "This isn't golf. This is a movement." energy

## Technical Constraints
- Static site: HTML + Tailwind CSS CDN + vanilla JS
- GitHub Pages hosting (no server-side)
- Must work on mobile (bottom nav bar pattern)
- Anime.js for animations
- Google Sheets integration for live data
